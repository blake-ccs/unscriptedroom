About The Unscripted Room

The room, the questions,
and the belief behind it.

Every element of this podcast was designed with intention — the time, the questions, the principles, and the host who holds it all together.

Time

Three hours.
One conversation.
No interruptions.

Most conversations today are rushed. Quick reactions, short clips, and surface-level exchanges have become the norm. The Unscripted Room takes a different approach.

Each episode unfolds over three uninterrupted hours, where a host and three guests stay with one conversation long enough for deeper thinking to emerge. Staying in the conversation moves guests beyond quick opinions into reflection, deeper curiosity, and genuine understanding.

Those who lean in often leave with something rare: deeper insight into their own thinking, greater respect for how others see the world, and the experience of being fully heard.
Questions

Nine questions.
Three themes.
Anywhere but simple.

Each episode of The Unscripted Room Podcast follows a sequence of nine open-ended questions. They guide the conversation through three themes: the lessons we carry from the past, how we act on what we understand today, and what we hope to do with the time we have ahead.

The questions themselves are simple. But when people stay with them long enough, the conversation becomes anything but.

In a world where many discussions are focused on proving who's right, The Unscripted Room explores something different: what we can learn from one another.

Our mission is ambitious: to give viewers a small escape from self-doubt by watching people have the courage to be themselves. When curiosity leads the conversation, people begin sharing their lived experiences that shape how they see the world. And sometimes, that's where the most meaningful insights appear.

Conversation Principles

Three principles.
One goal.

Great conversations don't happen by accident. In a world full of noise, quick reactions, and competing opinions, The Unscripted Room relies on a small set of principles that protect curiosity and encourage real listening.

Principle One

We strive to understand the difference between perspective and truth.

We listen and speak from lived experience. Our focus is understanding truth through the richness of differing perspectives.

Principle Two

We are all equals.

Equality is expressed through how we listen and how we learn from the wisdom in the room.

Principle Three

We choose curiosity over authority.

Our goal isn't to win. It's to understand. When we drift, we pause and return to curiosity.

When these principles are honored, they shape the tone of the conversation. They invite us to listen generously and share courageously.
Brett Host & Creator
Brett's story

The room began
with a belief.

Everyone knows something and deserves a dignified conversation to share it. That belief is what started The Unscripted Room — and it came from a very personal place.

Brett spent years working in coaching and training, building a career helping professionals in the automotive industry find their footing. Through his company CCS, he worked with over 5,000 professionals across the Jaguar Land Rover network — and what he found, again and again, was that people had remarkable things to say when someone gave them the space to say it.

That feeling disappears the moment an honest conversation begins.

But he also noticed something else: how easy it was to feel isolated in a singular perspective. To go through life feeling like your experience, your questions, your way of seeing things — were somehow yours alone. That feeling dissolves the moment an honest conversation begins.

The Unscripted Room is the first step in a larger effort to help people build what Brett calls a curiosity mindset — the habit of approaching the world, and the people in it, with genuine openness.
