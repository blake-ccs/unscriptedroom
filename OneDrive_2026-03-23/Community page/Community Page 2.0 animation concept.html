What is a Community Room

A live conversation
designed to go deeper.

If the podcast is where curiosity is shared, this is where it's practiced.

Join a live, hosted conversation designed to go deeper than small talk, with up to twelve guests. Guided by a sequence of nine open-ended questions and supported by simple conversation principles, each room creates space for people to think freely, listen generously, and explore ideas together.

The goal is simple: to see what becomes possible when a group of people gives their full attention to the same conversation.

How it works

Inside a
Community Room

Every room begins with a simple exchange: you offer your time and a willingness to share your perspective. In return, you gain access to the experiences and insights of others in the room.

A host introduces the conversation principles and begins the sequence of open-ended questions that guide the discussion. The questions give direction, but they never rush the conversation.

Some conversations move quickly through the questions. Others linger longer with a single idea.

The goal isn't to reach the final question. It's to create the best conversation the room can have.
